What we do

We build AI that fits how your organization actually works

We embed with your team, learn how your organization operates, and build AI tools tailored to your specific needs. Sometimes that means automating a process that's consuming too much staff time. Sometimes it means making sense of data that exists but isn't being used. The scope depends on what will make the most meaningful difference.

Rural India
The challenge

Community health workers in rural India were managing patient care largely through paper records and experience. Without a systematic way to assess risk across their caseload, identifying which patients needed urgent attention was difficult — and the consequences of missing someone were serious.

What we built

A risk prediction tool that draws on data the health workers were already collecting — blood pressure, blood glucose, pregnancy history, and age — and produces a real-time risk assessment for each patient. Designed to run on basic devices and fit naturally into their existing workflow.

Outcome
Frontline workers can now make better-informed decisions about who needs attention first.
How it works

From first conversation to finished tool

01

You reach out

A short form. Tell us about your organization and what you're dealing with.

02

We have a conversation

If there's something useful we can build, we set up a call. We ask a lot of questions before touching anything technical.

03

We define the problem together

We don't start building until we're both clear on what we're solving and why. This sometimes takes more than one conversation.

04

We build and test with you

We work iteratively and check in with your team throughout. You're involved from the start, not handed something at the end.

05

The tool is yours

We hand it over, make sure your team is comfortable using it, and stay available if anything needs adjusting.

Why free

Most AI tools aren't built with nonprofits in mind

Most sophisticated AI tools are built for organizations with significant technology budgets. Nonprofits are frequently left out of that picture. We don't think that's acceptable, so we don't charge.

There are no fees, no licensing costs, and no obligations.

"The organizations doing the most important work are usually the ones with the least resources for this kind of thing."
— Founder, AI with Purpose

Built around your workflows

We don't arrive with a product to sell. Whatever we build is designed from scratch around how your organization operates.

Capacity where it's needed

The goal is to reduce the administrative load on your team so they can spend more time on the work that actually matters.

A small number of projects at a time

We maintain a limited active project list so we can give each engagement the attention it warrants.

No obligations

If we're not the right fit for your organization right now, we'll say so directly. There's no pressure in either direction.
